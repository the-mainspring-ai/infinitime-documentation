Bonus Hours

 

Bonus hours allow you to give employees a given amount of hours on top of their work hours.

 

Bonus Hours - This amount of hours will be given to an employee on that particular day.  

 

Hours Required To Receive Bonus Hours - This amount is the minimum amount of hours an employee must work to receive the bonus hours.

 

Daily Max. After Bonus Hours are Awarded  – This is the amount of hours that an employee may work and receive bonus hours, but with bonus hours added, will not exceed this number.  The employee can still work over this hour amount, but it is earned and no bonus is added.

 

Example:

  • If an employee works Seven and 3/4 hours for that day, they receive 15 min of bonus hours.

  • But if the employee works Nine hours he will not get the bonus 15 min because he/she may not exceed nine hours in this example.
