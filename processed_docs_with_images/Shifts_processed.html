Shifts

The shift tab is used to assign shifts to an employee. Employees can be assigned to more than one shift. InfiniTime will automatically choose the appropriate shift according to the employee’s clock in time. Employees must fall within the grace periods set in their policy in order for their activity to be associated with a shift. For example, an employee clocks in 30 minutes prior to their shift. Grace Period settings are configured to consider this as an ‘Early’ punch. The employee’s time will be considered on the shift. Refer to Policy Setup for more information on grace periods.

 

 

Assigning a shift to an employee:

 

  1. Click Insert.

 

 

  1. Type the name of the shift you wish to assign into the box or use the Magnify Glass.
